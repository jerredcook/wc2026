All kickoff times are U.S. Eastern (ET), matched to the FOX / Telemundo broadcast schedule. Three late games kick off at 12:00 AM ET and roll past midnight into the next calendar day in Eastern (shown in gold, on their local match-night date): Australia–Türkiye (Jun 13 local → Jun 14 ET), Austria–Jordan (Jun 16 → Jun 17 ET), and Tunisia–Japan (Jun 20 → Jun 21 ET — the 1,000th match in World Cup history). Brazil–Haiti (Jun 19) is shown at 8:30 PM ET; a few outlets list it at 9:00 PM.

The Refresh latest scores button pulls current scores — including matches in progress (red LIVE badge + live minute) — from a public scoreboard when the page is served over http(s), or via Claude when open in Claude's view. A file opened straight from disk (file://) is blocked by the browser from making web requests, so it keeps the saved scores; the status line always tells you which it is.
